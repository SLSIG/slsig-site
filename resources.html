Resources

Simulation Resources

 

NLN SIRC (National League for Nursing Simulation Innovation Resource Center:
http://sirc.nln.org/ -- Collaboration between Laerdal and NLN. Offers online courses and discussion forums as well as research instruments
 
SSH (Society for Simulation in Healthcare):
http://www.ssih.org/ -- multidisciplinary society, holds annual conference. Provides Lab accreditation as well as individual certification (CHSE, etc.). Offers webinars, prep courses, and a journal. Online SimCenter directory available.
 
INACSL (International Nursing Association for Clinical Simulation & Learning):
http://www.inacsl.org/ -- Promotes research, created evidence based practice standards for simulation. Hosts annual conference for nursing simulation. Journal, webinars, and research grants, as well as mentorships.
 
Simghosts (The Gathering of Healthcare Simulation Technology Specialists):
http://www.simghosts.org/sim/default.asp -- Non-profit organization dedicated to supporting the growing international population of professionals operating medical simulation technology and spaces.
 
University of Washington:
http://collaborate.uw.edu/resources-and-publications/simulation-resources.html

http://collaborate.uw.edu/educators-toolkit/debriefing-tools/incite-debriefing-tool.html
Modeled on Debriefing with Good Judgment
