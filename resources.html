Simulation Resources

 

NLN SIRC (National League for Nursing Simulation Innovation Resource Center:
Collaboration between Laerdal and NLN. Offers online courses and discussion forums as well as research instruments
 
SSH (Society for Simulation in Healthcare):
A multidisciplinary society that holds annual conferences. Provides Lab accreditation as well as individual certification (CHSE, etc.). Offers webinars, prep courses, and a journal. Online SimCenter directory available.
 
INACSL (International Nursing Association for Clinical Simulation & Learning):
Promotes research, created evidence based practice standards for simulation. Hosts annual conference for nursing simulation. Journal, webinars, and research grants, as well as mentorships.
 
Simghosts (The Gathering of Healthcare Simulation Technology Specialists):
Non-profit organization dedicated to supporting the growing international population of professionals operating medical simulation technology and spaces.
 
Laerdal SUN (Simulation User Network):
A network of peers and experts in the realm of simulation, hosted by Laerdal. There are semi-annual conferences for simulation pedagogy and technology.
http://simulation.laerdal.com/forum/files/default.aspx

Simulation Technician Network:
A group that is a bridge between technology and faculty by providing a range of experiences with sim techs from all over the globe.
 
The Sim Tech:
A diverse collection of free, libre, and proprietary simulation resources including scenarios, multimedia, and links to other resources.
