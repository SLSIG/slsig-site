About

The Saint Louis Simulation Group (SLSIG) was spearheaded by several simulation experts in the St. Louis region who wanted to bring the simulation community together for collaboration, education and resources.

Our Mission

Support excellence in simulation facilitation through collaboration and sharing of best practices.

Our Vision

The SLSIG will foster collaboration, use of best practices through research, patient safety, and professional development among simulation facilitators in both clinical practice and education.
