Agendas

There are 2 types of zipfiles to make for agendas: pre and post meeting.

  • Premeeting contains all the paperwork sent out in the actual email itself.
  • Postmeeting one is to contain all the paperwork sent in the email, minus the last meeting summary and replacing it with the actual current meeting's summary. You also add any and all supplamental files from the meeting
